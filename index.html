Movie Reviews

Titanic

Released: 1997

Director: James Cameron

Review:

"Titanic" is a classic romantic drama that tells the tragic love story of Jack and Rose aboard the ill-fated ship. With breathtaking visuals and a compelling narrative, it's a must-watch for any movie lover.

Rocky

Released: 1976

Director: John G. Avildsen

Review:

"Rocky" is an iconic sports drama that follows the journey of Rocky Balboa, an underdog boxer, as he trains for a shot at the heavyweight championship. This film is a testament to determination and perseverance.
